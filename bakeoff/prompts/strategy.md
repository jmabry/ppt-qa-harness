### Churn Deep Dive — The Q3 Problem

Q3 churned ARR jumped to $500K from $300K in Q2. This is the single biggest concern.

| Churned Account | Segment | ARR Lost | Reason | Preventable? |
|----------------|---------|----------|--------|-------------|
| Apex Manufacturing | Enterprise | $145K | Acquired by competitor's customer — contract not renewed post-M&A | No |
| Precision Dynamics | Mid-Market | $62K | Switched to in-house analytics team; felt our platform was "overkill" | Partially — could have down-tiered |
| 4 SMB accounts | SMB | $118K total | Price sensitivity; 3 moved to cheaper competitor (DataForge); 1 went out of business | Yes — retention offer could have saved 2-3 |
| TechFab Solutions | Mid-Market | $48K | Poor implementation experience; never achieved time-to-value | Yes — CS failure |
| 3 SMB accounts | SMB | $82K total | Low usage (<10% feature adoption); never fully onboarded | Yes — onboarding gap |
| Consolidated Parts Co | Mid-Market | $45K | Budget cuts — eliminated analytics spend entirely | No |

**Analysis:** $290K of $500K churn (58%) was potentially preventable. Root causes: weak SMB onboarding, no down-tier option, and one CS failure.

## Board Asks

1. **APPROVE: $2.5M incremental investment in Customer Success** — hire 8 FTEs (4 onboarding specialists, 2 renewal managers, 1 SMB CS lead, 1 CS ops/analytics). Expected ROI: reduce preventable churn by 60% = save ~$700K ARR/year. Payback: 14 months. If we don't act, Q4 churn could reach $600K+ based on the at-risk pipeline above.

2. **APPROVE: Usage-based pricing tier for SMB** — $499/mo entry point (vs current $2.5K/mo minimum). Captures 60+ SMB prospects/quarter currently lost on price. Projected impact: +$1.2M new ARR in first 12 months; reduces SMB churn by giving a down-tier option. Risk: some existing SMBs may down-tier (estimated $200K ARR impact, net positive).

3. **DISCUSS: Series C timing** — current runway is 81 months but growth is accelerating. Should we raise in Q2 2027 from a position of strength ($22-25M ARR, improving unit economics) or wait for $30M ARR milestone? Board input on timing, target raise size, and investor targets requested.
