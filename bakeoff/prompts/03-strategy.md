# Prompt 3: Business Strategy

Create a 12-slide board-level presentation about "Q3 2026 Strategic Review" for a Series B SaaS company ($15M ARR, 120 employees). Include:

- Cover slide with company logo placeholder and board meeting date
- Executive summary: 3 key wins, 2 concerns, 1 decision needed
- Revenue dashboard: ARR growth, net retention, pipeline by segment
- Product update: shipped features, adoption metrics, roadmap priorities
- Go-to-market: CAC trends, channel performance, expansion revenue
- Customer health: NPS, churn analysis, top accounts at risk
- Team & hiring: headcount by function, open roles, attrition
- Competitive landscape: positioning vs 3 named competitors
- Financial outlook: burn rate, runway, path to profitability
- Key risks and mitigations
- Board asks: what you need from the board this quarter
- Appendix: detailed financials (can be dense)

Use a professional, investor-ready aesthetic. Conservative colors. Data-heavy slides should use charts and tables, not just bullets.
