# Prompt 1: Creative / Fun

Create an 8-slide presentation about "The Art of Homemade Pasta" for a community cooking night. Include:

- A visually striking cover slide
- Why fresh pasta beats dried (with specific comparisons)
- Equipment needed (what's essential vs nice-to-have)
- 3 pasta shapes to master: tagliatelle, ravioli, orecchiette — each with technique tips
- Common mistakes and how to fix them
- A closing slide with encouragement

Use a warm, earthy color palette. Make it look like something you'd see at a nice cooking school, not a corporate presentation.
